🌍 Why Handwashing Matters

Hand hygiene is the simplest, most effective way to prevent infections and the spread of disease. Studies show that proper handwashing can reduce the risk of respiratory and gastrointestinal infections by up to 50%. Despite its importance, millions still lack access to clean water and awareness about handwashing techniques.

🎯 Our Mission
